## Logo Usage

### Primary Logo
- Orange/yellow gradient circle with "E∞" symbol
- Use on dark backgrounds
- Minimum size: 32px

### Clear Space
Maintain clear space of at least 25% of the logo width on all sides.

### Don'ts
- Don't stretch or distort
- Don't change the colors
- Don't add effects (drop shadows, outlines)
- Don't place on busy backgrounds without contrast

## Voice & Tone

### Personality
- **Fun** but not immature
- **Confident** but not arrogant
- **Inclusive** — everyone is welcome
- **Memetic** — embrace humor
- **Transparent** — no hidden agendas

### Writing Style
- Use casual, approachable language
- Humor is encouraged
- Stay positive — no FUD, no financial advice
- Use emojis sparingly but effectively
- Keep it concise

### Example Do's
- "gm $EVERYTHING fam"
- "Why settle for something? 🔥"
- "The community decided. We listened."

### Example Don'ts
- "BUY NOW BEFORE IT'S TOO LATE!!!"
- "$EVERYTHING will 100x guaranteed"
- Any financial advice or price promises
